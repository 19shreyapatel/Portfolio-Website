Project Evaluating LLMs Using Construction Scenario Prompt

Brief: This project explores how different prompt styles and levels of contextual information affect the quality, relevance, and accuracy of AI-generated responses across various LLMs (GPT, Gemini, Grok, Claude). The focus is on prompt engineering and context design in construction-related scenarios to improve communication and productivity and to check how different LLMs respond to the same prompt.

Scenario: Draft a COR for relocating tray and 4 inch EMT at Area A-110. The work is performed during night hours.
